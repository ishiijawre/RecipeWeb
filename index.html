Gulab jamun

Ingredients

  • ¾ cup (100 grams) milk powder, unsweetened
  • ½ cup (60 grams) maida / plain flour
  • ½ tsp baking powder
  • 2 tbsp ghee / clarified butter
  • milk, for kneading ghee or oil, for frying

Recipe

  1. Mix milk powder, all-purpose flour, baking soda, and ghee to form a dough.
  2. Shape the dough into small balls.
  3. Deep-fry the balls on low heat until golden brown.
  4. Prepare sugar syrup with water, sugar, and cardamom.
  5. Soak the fried balls in the sugar syrup for 2-3 hours before serving.

Kaju katli

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cashew nuts (kaju)
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1/2 tsp ghee (optional)
  • Silver vark for decoration (optional)

Recipe

  1. Grind the cashews into a fine powder without over-grinding.
  2. In a pan, mix sugar and water to make a sugar syrup of one-string consistency.
  3. Add the cashew powder to the syrup and cook on low flame until it forms a dough.
  4. Grease a surface with ghee, roll the dough evenly, and let it cool.
  5. Cut into diamond shapes and decorate with silver vark if desired.
